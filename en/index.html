Features

Everything you need to cut

From cut-and-join to subtitles, image layers and transitions — all on a Final Cut–style timeline.

✂️

Cut & join

Trim ranges into clips and stitch them in order. Trim handles, blade, fast copy cut.

⬚

Range select

Drag on the timeline to keep only a span or delete it. Clips snap together magnetically.

💬

Subtitles

Drag captions onto the video with live preview. Adjust size, color and outline.

🖼

Image layers

Drop PNG/JPG, drag & resize. Animate start→end as a moving route marker.

🎞

Transitions & fade

Dissolve, slide and fade between clips; fade in/out at the start and end.

▶

Sequence playback

Program monitor, smooth playhead, zoom, skim and a layer track.

🔒

Private by design

Zero uploads, zero servers. Your video is processed only inside your browser.

⚡

FFmpeg.wasm

Native codecs via WebAssembly. Multi-threaded where cross-origin isolated.

How it works

Three steps, done

1

Import

Drag & drop a video, or pick a file. It's not an upload — the browser reads it directly.

2

Edit

Drag to select spans to keep or cut, stitch clips, and check it with the playhead.

3

Export

One click outputs an mp4 to download. The file never leaves your device.

🔒

Your video never leaves your device

No cloud uploads, no backend servers. Everything runs inside your browser, so you can edit even sensitive footage with peace of mind.
